Put your money where it belongs.

envelope puts you at peace with your finances. Each month, assign your monthly income to various envelopes. You will know how much money you have left.

If you can't cover everything, then readjust the budgeted amount from an envelope to another. If this still doesn't work, no problem! Just substract what you need now from next month's envelope.
